Questions

Good to know

How do I place an order?
Simply tap any "Order on WhatsApp" button. It opens a chat with us where you can send your photos and tell us the occasion. We'll guide you from there.
What photos work best?
Clear, well-lit photos look best. Send the highest quality version you have — straight from your phone gallery is perfect. We'll let you know if anything needs adjusting.
Can I see a preview before printing?
Yes! We share a digital preview of the design and only print once you're happy with it.
Do you deliver outside Hubli?
We're based in Hubli and can arrange delivery to nearby areas and beyond. Message us on WhatsApp with your location and we'll sort out the details.
How long does it take?
Most orders are designed within a day and ready shortly after. Turnaround can vary with quantity — we'll always give you a clear timeline up front.
